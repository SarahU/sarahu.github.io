Coaching

For those looking for longer term training, confidence building or general technical career coaching, I offer weekly, fortnightly, or monthly coaching.
Cost: 50min session at £25 (Associates and Interns), £35 (Mid-level), £45 (Senior+).
Coaching is aimed at those currently working and aiming to improve skills for promotions. Those not working, and looking for assistance to get a new job, can sign-up for mentorship above.

You can book a free initial discussion with me here


The difference between my Coaching and Mentorship is:
With Mentorship: Topics and sessions are driven by the mentee & we might not have regular sessions, but it is always free.
With Coaching: we have regular, prepared sessions, that move you forward, toward completing specific goals; I follow up with you; send reminders where required; and will do work on your behalf such as: creating a CV, searching for jobs, reviewing applications, reviewing code in projects, etc.
